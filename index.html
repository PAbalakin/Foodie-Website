My Top Three Foods

1


Cookie Butter Ice Cream

Cookie Butter ice cream is 500,000$ worth of flavor crammed into a tin worth a mere 15$ at Trader Joes. The flavor is exactly what it sounds like. A rich creamy spread made from European spice cookies called speculoos cookies. This ice cream is a successfull attempt to bring that sweet, rich flavor into the frozen world, blending delicious vanilla ice cream with generous swirls of actual cookie butter.

2

Sticky Thai Chicken Wings

Wings is a staple food in the American diet, but we've never tried wings like this. From the moment you take your first bite, you'll be transported into the bustling streets of Thailand, surrounded by the tantalizing aromas of fragrant spices and exotic flavors. The crispy, golden-brown exterior of the wings gives way to tender, juicy meat that practically falls off the bone. These wings are a true masterpiece of flavor, and each bite is like a symphony of tastes and textures.

3

Crepes

A traditional Russian food, Crepes are so great, because they can be eaten with anything. Do you have a sweet tooth or are you craving something more savory. Good for you because, the flavor of the crepes depends on your choice of filling. They're made with simple, wholesome ingredients like flour, eggs, and milk, so they're the quick to make and inexpensive. They are also delicous by themselves, but crepes with nutella filling is one of the most delictable and buttery treats of all time cementing it in the top three food hall of fame.
